Curriculum

The goal is not just learning AI.
It is applying AI to your own work.

The class focuses on practice and concrete outputs. You leave with a working tool, a prompt structure, and an operating model you can reuse.

01
Build work tools with vibe coding
Break down a workflow into screens and functions, then build a simple tool or automation flow with AI.
02
Turn prompts into operating routines
Design reusable routines for meetings, proposals, customer management, and other repeated tasks.
03
Define agent roles and knowledge structures
Clarify what AI should handle, what knowledge it should reference, and where humans approve.
Class Flow

How the class works

We focus on turning your work into a small AI-supported system rather than promising instant full automation.

Choose work
Pick a repeated or time-consuming real task.
Map flow
Separate inputs, decisions, outputs, and approvals.
Design AI
